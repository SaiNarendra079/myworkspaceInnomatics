Conditional Rendering-
   it is the process of displaying some content(components,jsx, elements) on the screen depending upon certain conditions

in js conditional statement are if else,while,do while,switch
four ways of applying  conditional rendering in react:
    --if else 
    --using element variable
    --using ternary operator
    --short circuit operator
